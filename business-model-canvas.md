## 1. Customer Segments

**Primary — Prop Challenge Takers (Retail Traders)**
- Traders actively attempting FTMO, FundedNext, The5ers, or similar evaluations
- Age 22–40, globally distributed (EU, Asia, LATAM heavy)
- Repeat buyers: 80–90% fail and retry; average trader attempts 2–4 challenges
- High intent, high urgency: a rule breach costs real money ($100–$2,000)
- Pain: prop firm rules are complex, change without notice, and vary by plan
- Reached via: SEO (problem searches), Discord communities, Reddit, educator referrals

**Secondary — New Prop Firm Researchers**
- Traders comparing firms before their first challenge purchase
- High value as affiliate conversion targets
- Reached via: SEO (comparison searches), Reddit, YouTube discovery

**Tertiary — Trading Educators / Coaches**
- Use FreeTraderHub tools in their teaching content
- Potential B2B or partner tier for Course (Stream 4) co-distribution
- High influence multiplier: one educator endorsement = 500–5,000 qualified visitors
- Reached via: direct outreach (see `traffic-growth-plan.md` Channel 5)

## 2. Value Propositions

**For Challenge Takers:**
- Eliminate preventable rule breaches with precision tools (Position Sizer, Loss Limit Tracker)
- Model challenge success probability before risking money (Monte Carlo simulator)
- Single source of truth for firm rules — neutral, independent, regularly updated
- Rule-change alerts that protect funded accounts in real time (Pro tier)

**For Researchers:**
- Side-by-side firm comparisons with no sales agenda
- Plain-English rule explanations that prop firms themselves don't provide
- Best-firm-for-X-strategy guides backed by actual rule data

**For Educators:**
- Ready-made tools their students actually need
- Co-branded content opportunities
- Affiliate income for course/tool referrals

**Unique Positioning Statement:**
> "The only prop firm resource where the tools are built by traders, not marketers."

## 4. Customer Relationships

**Self-service (dominant):**
Tools are self-serve, no account required. Reduces friction, builds habit and return visits.

**Community:**
- Email newsletter: recurring relationship via "weekly rule brief"
- Trading Discord presence: active participation, not broadcast
- Pro subscriber community (Phase 3 option)

**Trust-maintenance protocol:**
- No affiliate link without disclosure label
- Rule library updated within 48 hours of any firm rule change
- Editorial corrections published publicly
- No sponsored content that could compromise rule data neutrality

## 7. Key Activities

**Core (must maintain):**
- Rule library accuracy and 48-hour update SLA
- Tool reliability and performance
- Editorial independence (never compromise rule data for revenue)

**Growth (add in Phase 1–2):**
- SEO content production: 8–10 programmatic pages + 1 editorial piece per month
- Community participation: Discord and Reddit engagement (not broadcasting)
- Email list growth: capture CTAs on every tool and content page
- Affiliate program management: apply, track, optimise placements

**Scale (Phase 2–3):**
- Pro feature development (auth, billing, saved sessions, multi-firm comparison)
- Educator partnership outreach (5 targets per month)
- Course production (only after waitlist validation)
- Firm expansion: add 5–9 more firms to rule library

**Ongoing:**
- Trust maintenance: disclose everything, update rules fast, fix errors publicly
- Email nurture: value-first sequence, never spam

## 9. Cost Structure

**Fixed (Current):**
- Domain + hosting: ~$20–$50/month
- Tool maintenance (founder time): low

**Variable (Growth Phase):**
- SEO content production: $500–$2,000/month (freelance or AI-assisted)
- Email platform: $29–$99/month (scales with list size)
- Community management time: 2–4 hours/week (founder or VA)
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Stripe)

**One-time (Product Build):**
- Pro tool tier: $500–$2,000 if outsourced (auth + billing + feature gates)
- Course production: $200–$500 in tooling (Loom, Podia/Gumroad)
- Media kit design: $100–$300 (for sponsored listings pitch)

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $0 (organic-only strategy — no paid acquisition)
- LTV/CAC ratio: effectively infinite at zero acquisition cost
- Gross margin: 100% on affiliate, 85%+ on subscriptions, 70%+ on course
- Break-even: Month 1 (affiliate commissions cover hosting costs immediately)
