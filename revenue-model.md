## Platform Summary

FreeTraderHub.com is a precision toolkit for proprietary trading firm challenge takers.
Current state: three free tools (Position Sizer, Loss Limit Tracker, Monte Carlo Pass
Probability), a rule library covering 94 rules across 13 plans at FTMO, FundedNext, and
The5ers, and a "no-pitch" educational blog. Zero revenue. Strong trust positioning.

## Strategic Position

FreeTraderHub has built something rare in a noisy, affiliate-saturated niche: a
trust-first brand. The "no pitch" signal and absence of visible monetisation have
created a quality halo. This is a competitive moat — but also leaving significant
revenue on the table.

The monetisation strategy must preserve the trust asset while unlocking revenue.
The rule: **every monetization layer must add value, not extract it.**

### North Star Principle
> Monetise the outcome (passing the challenge), not the audience.

## Revenue Architecture

Three tiers designed to respect user trust at each level:

```
Tier 1 — Disclosed Referrals (passive, low friction)
  └─ Affiliate links to prop firms, clearly labelled
  └─ "Best firm for X strategy" comparison guides
  └─ Feeds from: SEO (comparison keywords), Email list

Tier 2 — Tool Upgrades (pull-based, high value)
  └─ Pro tool tier with advanced analytics
  └─ Firm rule-change alert subscription
  └─ Feeds from: Direct tool users, Discord power users, Email list

Tier 3 — Knowledge Products (high margin, opt-in)
  └─ Challenge prep course
  └─ Sponsored firm placements (clearly labelled)
  └─ Feeds from: Email list (1,000+ required), Educator partnerships
```

## Trust-Safe Monetisation Rules

1. **Affiliate links must be disclosed** — always label "affiliate" or "we may earn
   a commission". Rankings driven by data, not commission size.

2. **No editorial-for-hire** — never accept payment to change a review verdict.
   Sponsored content is clearly labelled and segregated from rule/tool content.

3. **Pro tools must be genuinely better** — the free tier stays free and useful.
   No artificial crippling of free tools to force upgrades.

4. **Rule library stays neutral** — firm comparison data is factual regardless
   of affiliate relationships. This is the core trust asset.

5. **Email list is opt-in, value-first** — the "weekly brief on rule changes" is a
   genuine product. Monetise it with relevant offers, not spam sequences.
