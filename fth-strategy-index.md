## The Three Rules

1. **Traffic gates revenue.** No traffic plan = no revenue plan.
   Hit the traffic milestones in `traffic-growth-plan.md` before expecting
   the revenue numbers in `revenue-model.md` to materialise.

2. **Email list is the master lever.** Every decision about content, tools, and
   community should be evaluated by: does this grow the email list?
   See the list-size unlock table in `roadmap.md`.

3. **Trust is the moat.** Every affiliate link is disclosed. Rule data is never
   compromised by commercial relationships. Pro tools are genuinely better,
   not artificially gated. One broken trust signal undoes everything.
